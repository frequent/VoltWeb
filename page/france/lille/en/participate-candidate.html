Be a Candidate

A Citizen List

Our goal of establishing politics as citizen responsibility of course also implies participating in elections. As we are aiming for citizen representation we are not limited by the size of our local teams - as intended by our motto "you are part of it" - our idea is to assemble a list of qualified citizens, who share our values and are willing to run as candidates for Volt. We are not looking for political acumen, but citizens vested in their communities, with or without experience, but aware of what is working in Lille and what needs to be improved. If you care for Lille and the community you live in and also believe democracy should mean more than merely voting, how about volunteering as a candidate for us?

What are the Requirements to be a candidate?

  • You need to eligible to stand for elections, meaning you need to be an EU citizen, be at least 18 years old and you have to live in Lille.
  • You need to adhere to the core values of Volt.

How do Candidates get selected and for what Position on the list?

All applications will be reviewed regarding eligibility. The placement of all candidates on the list will then be determined by a democratic vote among official Volt Lille members (join us and become a member).

What documents do I have to provide?

Documents shared with members of Volt Lille to help them decide during the placement vote:

  • a candidate letter (explaining the reasons you want to become candidate and what you think you can add to our campaign)
  • your resume

Declaration documents that will only be submitted to the electoral committee of Volt Lille

  • Declaration of honor to become member of Volt France and to stand for and defend our programme if you are chosen to as candidat(template)
  • Declaration of prior political affiliation with other parties/movements (template)
  • Declaration of previous and ongoing judicial convictions (template)
