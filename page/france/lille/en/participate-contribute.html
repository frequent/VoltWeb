Contribute an idea

You have a proposal for someting to improve in your community ? Please let us know. We would like to collect all ideas and suggestions, both big and small, to evaluate and better understand the bigger picture. We see our role first and foremost as listeners and faciliators, that on one side would like to find out what matters to each and every one and on the other side try to moderate the information we receive and - if feasible and conforming with our core values - develop them into proper and sophisticated policies. Your contributions are the first step on this way.

Please use the form below to send us your ideas and suggestions. The submit button will open your email editor, where you can also add attachments if you like. We will review all submissions and publish them fully or partially to improve our collection of existing proposals.
