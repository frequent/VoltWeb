Donation and Transparancey Guidelines

Volt France is committed to upholding high ethical values, standards and transparency beyond legal requirements.

In doing so, please note that we will voluntarily publish donations exceeding 3000€ within a calendar year. With your acknowledgement you support our efforts to maintaining political independence.

For more information, please refer to our Donation and Transparency guidelines

Why are my personal information required?

French law requires that all donators have to be identifiable. Without personal information, we are neither permitted to accept a donation nor able to issue the mandatory donation receipts.

Please also note, that we are only permitted to accept donations from EU citizens living in France or French citizens living abroad, that the maximum amount of donations made to political parties cannot exceed 7500€ per year and that tax receipts can only be issued to the person having made the donation.

Tax Deductability

The French law renders donations to political parties made by citizens to be tax deductable up to certain amounts - currently 66% of donations capped at 20% of overall income. For more information on how to file for tax deducation, please refer to the pages of the French public service or get in touch with us.
