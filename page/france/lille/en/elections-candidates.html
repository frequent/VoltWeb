Our Candidates

A Citizen List

An (almost) empty list ? Who are our candidates ? This is our project for the next 10 months. Our idea is to involve citizens more actively in politics and our parole for Lille is called "You are part of the puzzle", so yes - it's you - one of our potential candidates.

We're looking for citizens of Lille who are willing to step forward to represent their communities and to become candidates either on a representative positions at the end of the list or on a position which might get you elected.

We are humble and will consider it a big success if we manage to assemble "the 61" to be able to participate in the elections - either by ourselves or together with other citizen movements. Then getting into the second round and possibly elected will be another story altogether. We chase our dreams, but we are also realistic. Being an actual candidate for us means a lot of work with a miniscule chance of success. But to us it seems these times are manifesting themselves with seemingly unsurmountable challenges and yet - we try. Join us.

The 61

In alphabetical order. The final position on the list will be determined by a vote among members of Volt Lille.
